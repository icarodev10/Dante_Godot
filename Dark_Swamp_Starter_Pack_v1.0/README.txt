=== CONTENTS ===
RPG Maker:
- Tileset A1: 3-frame animated water terrain
- Tileset A2: 12 grass tiles
- Tileset A5: 2 solid color tiles for background fill
- Tileset B: 5 environment props, 4 bridge variations (side view versions can be custom shaped and stacked), 3 water-muck detail tiles, 1 static altar image
- Altar Spritesheet: 4 frames of animation

Raw Assets:
- 4 tileset PNGs, cropped for flexible, engine-agnostic use
- 4 individual images of altar frames for maximum flexibility

=== USAGE TIPS ===
In General
- Grass tiles can be used over a solid-colored background or autotiled:
    - RPG Maker: Use Tileset A5 for solid grass/water tiles and add details from A2/B on top
    - Other engines/frameworks: Either use these solid-color tiles or match these background colors:
        Grass - HEX: #345318 | RGB: (52, 83, 24)
        Water - HEX: #112725 | RGB: (17, 39, 37)

RPG Maker
- Place the tilesets in img/tilesets
- Place the altar spritesheet in img/characters, keeping the “!$” prefix for correct rendering
- Separate altar frame images are in the RawAssets folder for event use
- A static altar tile is included in Tileset B

Other Engines/Frameworks
- When slicing tiles, use 24x24 instead of 48x48 if your engine does not support autotiling
- Scale tiles up (not down) for best image quality
- For smoother water animation, duplicate the 2nd tileset group and use it as a fourth frame in the animation loop
